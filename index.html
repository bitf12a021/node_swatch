About Us

Swatch Bharat (SB) is a mission for promoting Clean India where restaurants ,places of public interest , temples , Bus stands and Railway stations are, maintained clean , To achieve the level of interest in cleanliness, people’s participation is necessary. So the goal of this project is to collect feed back from people who visit these places in each city and present the results to the concerned authorities for necessary steps to be taken to improve/remedy the situation . People’s participation in feedback is the ultimate goal .
